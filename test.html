Use this local environment to load, test, and verify your Chrome extension.

How to Load and Test the Extension:

  1. Open your Google Chrome browser and navigate to chrome://extensions/.
  2. In the top-right corner, toggle the **"Developer mode"** switch to **ON**.
  3. In the top-left corner, click **"Load unpacked"**.
  4. Select the extension directory:
    C:\Users\prati\dev\swift-query
  5. Once loaded, refresh this page (F5).
  6. Try highlighting or selecting text in the paragraph below to trigger the popup!

Features to Verify:

  • Theme Toggle: Click the extension icon in the Chrome toolbar. Toggle the theme between Light/Dark/System. The settings window and floating popup will update instantly.
  • Trigger Modes:
    • Instant: Highlights show the engine list immediately.
    • Search Circle: Highlights show a small search icon bubble. Clicking it reveals the engine choices.
    • Key Shortcut: Selection only triggers if you hold Ctrl, Alt, or Shift while selecting.
  • Engine Filter: Turn off Claude or Gemini in settings. They should immediately disappear from the floating popup.
  • Automated Injections: Click "ChatGPT", "Gemini", or "Claude" inside the popup. A new tab will open, the text will inject into their prompt field, and if "Auto-Submit" is enabled, the search will initiate automatically!
